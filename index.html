Our Facilities

World Class Library

The William Oxley Thompson Memorial Library is the main library at Ohio State University's Columbus campus. It is the university's largest library.

Recreational Center

Ohio State’s largest recreation facility, the RPAC, is a multi-purpose facility complex that combines innovative fitness spaces, located in the heart of campus.

Short North

Short North Arts District is a hip, culture-rich area centered on N. High Street. Contemporary galleries fill converted warehouses, and the Wexner Center for the Arts hosts boundary-pushing exhibitions.
